Slay the Spire

Slay the Spire is singleplayer deck-building game with roguelike element. The game focuses on three character with their own combat style show through the deckbuilding element of the game. This game is released on Microsoft Windows, macOS, Linux, PlayStation 4 and Nintendo Switch.

Enter the Gungeon

Enter the Gungeon is a bullet hell roguelike video game developed by Dodge Roll and published by Devolver Digital. It follows four adventurers as they descend into the Gungeon to find a gun to kill their past.
